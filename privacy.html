Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to respond to your enquiry. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

1. Data we collect

When you submit an enquiry or book a taster session through this website, we collect the following information:

  • Contact details — your name, work email address, and company name
  • Enquiry details — your sector, team size, and the message you write to us
  • Technical data — your IP address and browser type, collected automatically by our form provider (Formspree) and hosting platform (GitHub Pages)

We do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data, or any information about minors. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

Form submissions

Form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party service. By submitting a form, your data passes through Formspree's servers before being delivered to us by email. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to this processing and can be found at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

2. How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or taster session booking
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Send you information you have specifically requested

We will never use your data for automated marketing, sell it to third parties, or share it with any organisation outside of what is strictly necessary to deliver our service.

Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to your direct enquiry) and, where you have ticked the consent checkbox on our form, on the basis of explicit consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

3. Data storage & retention

Your data is stored in our email inbox (Google Workspace) and is not held in any additional CRM or database unless you become a client, at which point we may keep records necessary for delivering our service and meeting legal obligations.

We retain enquiry data for a maximum of 24 months from the date of your last contact with us, after which it is permanently deleted.

Our email provider (Google) may process your data on servers located in the United States. Google operates under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, providing an adequate level of protection.

4. Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Formspree — form submission processing (formspree.io)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting (github.com)
  • hCaptcha — bot protection on our contact form (hcaptcha.com)

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other advertising or tracking technology on this site.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can ask for your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
