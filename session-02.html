In-session exercises

Four exercises.
All real work.

Everything uses your actual content. Generic examples tell you nothing — your own work teaches you everything.

01
Bullets to brief
Each person provides five rough bullet points about a real topic. Using the expand-from-bullets method, they produce a full professional draft in under three minutes. The gap between effort and output is genuinely surprising.
Drafting drill
02
Style brief builder
Participants paste three examples of their existing best writing into a prompt template. AI analyses the tone and produces a style brief. They use it immediately to rewrite something generic into their own voice.
Brand voice
03
The long-document sprint
Each person brings (or is given) a 10-page document. Using three different summarisation prompts, they extract: a headline summary, a list of action items, and answers to three specific questions. All three done in under 10 minutes.
Summarisation
04
The draft challenge
One real document. Thirty minutes. Each person completes a first draft of something that's been sitting on their desk. Group critique at the end focuses on what AI got right — and what needed human editing.
