Writing, Faster

Session Description

This session focuses on effective writing for emails, proposals, and reports, providing participants with essential skills to communicate clearly and efficiently.

Agenda

  1. Introduction
  2. 90-Second Email
  3. Proposal Skeleton
  4. Voice Editing
  5. Break
  6. Writing Sprint

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the importance of concise communication.
  • Learn structure and format for effective proposals.
  • Enhance writing skills through practice and feedback.

Exercises

  • Write a concise email based on a given scenario.
  • Create a template for a proposal.
  • Participate in a voice editing exercise.
  • Engage in a writing sprint, focusing on speed and clarity.

Takeaways

  • The importance of clarity in written communication.
  • Tools for editing and improving writing.
  • Strategies for managing writing time effectively.
  • Inspiration to write regularly and clearly.
