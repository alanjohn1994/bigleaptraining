In-session exercises

Real documents.
Real data. Real answers.

Every exercise uses content that's directly relevant to your team's work.

01
The clause hunt
Given a 15-page sample contract, teams must find the answers to 10 specific questions using AI — without reading the document. Average time: 4 minutes. Without AI: 35+ minutes.
Document Q&A
02
Data in plain English
Teams paste a quarterly revenue spreadsheet into AI and ask five business questions in natural language. No formulas. No pivot tables. Just answers — and follow-up questions until the picture is clear.
Data analysis
03
Spot the fabrication
We present three AI-generated research summaries. Two are accurate. One contains a subtly fabricated statistic that sounds completely plausible. Teams must identify it using the verification checklist.
Critical thinking
04
The 30-minute brief
Teams use AI to produce a structured research brief on a topic relevant to their sector — competitive landscape, regulatory change, market sizing. Something that would typically take half a day.
